APPSTATE TUTORIAL FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Facebook AppState extraction and automated sharing

Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Facebook AppState?

Facebook AppState is a collection of cookies and tokens that authenticate your Facebook session. It allows automated tools to interact with Facebook on your behalf without needing your username and password.

Why do I need to extract my AppState?

Extracting your AppState allows you to use automated tools for tasks like sharing posts, managing groups, or other Facebook activities without manual intervention. It's particularly useful for social media managers or marketers.

Is this method against Facebook's Terms of Service?

Automating interactions with Facebook may violate their Terms of Service. We recommend using this only for legitimate purposes and being aware that Facebook may detect and restrict automated activity. Always use responsibly.

Technical Issues

Why do I need Kiwi Browser?

Kiwi Browser is one of the few Android browsers that supports Chrome extensions, which is necessary to install the FBState Utility tool needed to extract your AppState.

The extension isn't showing up after installation

Try these steps:

  1. Make sure Developer Mode is enabled in Kiwi Browser's extensions page
  2. Restart Kiwi Browser
  3. Check if the extension appears when you tap the 3-dot menu
  4. Try reinstalling the extension

My AppState stopped working after a few days

Facebook AppStates typically expire after a few days (usually 2-7 days). You'll need to extract a fresh AppState when this happens. This is a security measure by Facebook.

Safety Concerns

Is it safe to share my AppState?

No! Your AppState is like a digital key to your Facebook account. Anyone with your AppState can access your account just as if they had your password. Only use it with trusted tools and never share it publicly.

Should I use my main Facebook account?

We strongly recommend using a dummy account for testing and automation purposes. This protects your main account from potential restrictions or security issues.

What should I do if I accidentally shared my AppState?

Immediately:

  1. Change your Facebook password
  2. Log out of all sessions from Facebook's security settings
  3. Revoke any suspicious apps in your Facebook settings
  4. Generate a new AppState if you still need one
